A Successful Partnership

CHW is a consulting firm with a global reach that specializes in economic growth and development, providing top quality research products, books, videos, and training materials. Our experienced team has collectively worked in over 30 large scale research projects and has published countless reports and books for government institutions, multilateral bodies, and companies.

Research

Our core team of economic growth and development researchers will deliver top research products for informed decisions using an effective workflow and the best tools and techniques available. And our network of consultants allows us to work in all areas of economic growth and development.

Long-term solutions

We are committed to developing systems and processes in order to provide long-term solutions to development problems. From policy design to legal recommendations we are constantly building systems and managing our knowledge base in order not to reinvent the wheel with every project.

Dissemination and Training

From findings to action, solutions and recommendations require an incidence strategy. Our knowledge base allows us to train and inform all stakeholders involved in our projects through books, seminars, technical training, online courses, and more.
